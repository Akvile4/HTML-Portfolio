TOP 3 Movies by AKVILE

My top 3 movies of all time, that I would recomment to everyone to watch!

#1
Coroline

This is the best animated movie of all time. It's a bit scary as for kids, but absolutely worth watching. The graphics are fantastic and the amount time spent to make this movie is insane!

IMDB Rating: 7.7/10

#2
The Dressmaker

This is one of the best movies of all time. It has everything in it: a lot of drama, thriller, a little bit of horror, some comedy bits and my favourite part - detective. But I am lying, the best part is has Kate Winslet in it and she is absolutely amazing!

IMDB Rating: 7/10

#3
Harry Potter SAGA

I love all Harry Potter movies and I can watch them again and again. We have a Christmas tradition to watch all movies but sometimes we do that even in the summer. They are just so amazing and even they are family movies they have quite a lot of darkness in them!

IMDB Rating: 8/10
